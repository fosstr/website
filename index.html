FOSSTR

Fostering Free and Open Source Software!

Objectives


  • 1. Education (Learn and Share)
    • 1.1 To share the empowerment & freedoms that FOSS brings
    • 1.2 To organise study-programs and events on various types of FOSS solutions

  • 2. Advocacy
    • 2.1 Encourage use of FOSS in organizations
    • 2.2 Encourage use of FOSS for personal use
    • 2.3 Encourage use of open standards for document exchange

  • 3. Contribution
    • 3.1 Active: code, patch, assist with localisation and documentation
    • 3.2 Passive: give feedback received from community to developers
